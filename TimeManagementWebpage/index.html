Discern Essential from Non-essential

Use a tool to find out what is really important and needs to get done

Sometimes, prioritizing is difficult. Therefore, tools sometimes make the process easier by applying a set of standards to each priority and evaluating without bias or unreal expectations.

Setting Boundaries

Use goal setting to help limit your "Yes"s and "No"s and increase the likelihood of success

It's proven that by setting mini-goals you are more likely to achieve your goals. However, there are some techniques that already include this and are said to be even more effective, like SMART goals.

Dare to Say No

Be strong and don't budge on certain "No"s

Discipline is difficult as it is. Saying "No" shouldn't be made tough. Most times, it should be effortless and free and doubt.

Journal

Reflect and adapt to what works and doesn't work

How are you expected to know what to change or how you've changed without putting a focus on how you've adapted? Using a self-selected method to reflect can be a game-changer for acknowledging those small wins or losses and using them to build better strategies for success.
